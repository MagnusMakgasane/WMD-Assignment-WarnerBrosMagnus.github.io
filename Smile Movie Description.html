Smile

The ending of the 2022 horror movie Smile sees Joel (Kyle Gallner) arriving at Rose's childhood home and realizing that she is too far gone to help. Rose sees the monster, has a mental breakdown, and can't move. She truly and fully embodies the curse by starting a fire with her own body. Joel realizes too late that Rose has given him the curse. It's an effective ending since it doesn't seem that Rose would be able to survive this. In Smile, characters pass the curse on to each other when they kill themselves in front of someone else, which is much darker than most horror movies about deadly and violent curses. The ending of the film is particularly sad since it proves that as soon as Joel agrees to help Rose figure out what's happening, he isn't able to extract himself from her, and he will never break free. It's amazing to think that Joel makes the seemingly small decision to be there for Rose and this is what happens. The Smile ending proves that not only does the film have some of the scariest horror movie grins, but there's a solid story being told as well. Rose is different from other horror movie characters as there is a reason why she is being stalked by an entity and it has to do with her difficult and upsetting past.
